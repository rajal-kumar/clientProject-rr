Dr. Rish Ram - General Surgeon

Fellow of The Royal Australasian College of Surgeons Member of the Endocrine Section of The Australasian College of Surgeons Member of Australasian & New Zealand Gastric and Oesophageal Surgeons Association

What I Do

My current practice is as a General and Upper GI Surgeon both in the private sector and public sector at Auckland Hospital. My areas of expertise are laparoscopic hernia repair, including ventral and groin hernias and laparoscopic cholecystectomy (gall stones).

Other areas of expertise include stomach and bowel surgery, varicose vein and skin cancer surgery, hemorrhoids and pilonidal sinus.

I am a Southern Cross Affiliated Provider for initial consultation, hernia, gallbladder, varicose veins, skin lesions, fine needle biopsies, cryotherapy, drainage of abscesses, ingrown toenails and injection of hemorrhoids.
